# Rakshak AI — Demo Video Script (2 min 30 sec)

## Format
- **Style**: Cinematic storytelling + screen recording overlay
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 landscape (YouTube-ready)
- **Audio**: Hindi (primary) with English subtitles
- **Tone**: Urgent → Hopeful → Inspiring
- **Music**: Starts tense, builds to hopeful resolution

---

## Scene 1: The Problem (0:00–0:30)

**Visual**: Black screen → fade to newspaper headlines
- "Bihar Floods: 50 villages cut off, no connectivity"
- "Uttarakhand Landslide: Rescue hindered by lack of communication"
- "Cyclone in Odisha: Survivors wait hours for medical aid"

**Voiceover (Hindi)**: 
> "भारत में हर साल हजारों लोग आपदाओं का सामना करते हैं। जब नेटवर्क खत्म हो जाता है, तो मदद भी खत्म हो जाती है।"

**[English subs]**: "Every year, thousands face disasters across India. When the network goes down, help goes down too."

**Cut to**: Priya's photo montage (NCC uniform, village background)
> "प्रिया से मिलिए। 19 साल की एनसीसी कैडेट। बिहार के मुज़फ़्फ़रपुर की रहने वाली।"

*"Meet Priya. 19, NCC cadet from Muzaffarpur, Bihar."*

---

## Scene 2: The Disaster (0:30–0:50)

**Visual**: Animated flood water rising, phone battery dropping
- Screen recording begins: Priya's phone home screen → opens Rakshak AI

**Sound**: Heartbeat, rain, tension

**Voiceover**: 
> "बाढ़ ने सब कुछ तबाह कर दिया। नेटवर्क खत्म। अस्पताल 40 किलोमीटर दूर। 200 से ज़्यादा लोग मदद के लिए तरस रहे हैं।"

*"The flood destroyed everything. No network. Hospital 40 km away. 200+ people desperate for help."*

**On screen**: Battery 12%, "No Signal" icon, panic

---

## Scene 3: Using Rakshak AI — Triage (0:50–1:30)

**Visual**: Screen recording — Medical Triage screen

**Voiceover**:
> "प्रिया Rakshak AI खोलती है। इंटरनेट की ज़रूरत नहीं। जेम्मा 4 AI डिवाइस पर ही काम करता है।"

*"Priya opens Rakshak AI. No internet needed. Gemma 4 AI runs entirely on-device."*

**Screen recording shows**:
1. Tapping "MEDICAL TRIAGE" card
2. Typing in Hindi: *"सांस नहीं ले रहा, नब्ज़ कमज़ोर, पैर में चोट"*
3. Tapping "AI Analysis" 
4. Loading spinner → Result: **IMMEDIATE (RED)** — "Start rescue breathing, control bleeding"

**Voiceover**:
> "वह हिंदी में लिखती है। AI तुरंत विश्लेषण करता है: RED — IMMEDIATE। तुरंत इलाज चाहिए।"

*"She types in Hindi. The AI instantly analyzes: RED — IMMEDIATE. Needs urgent care."*

5. Toggle switches: Walking → NO, Breathing → YES, etc.
6. START protocol runs → YELLOW / GREEN tags for others

> "47 मरीज़ों को महज़ 2 घंटे में ट्राएज किया। 100% ऑफलाइन। 100% AI-पावर्ड।"

*"47 patients triaged in just 2 hours. 100% offline. 100% AI-powered."*

---

## Scene 4: Saving Lives (1:30–1:50)

**Visual**: Split screen — Triage log on left, animated NDRF boat arriving on right

**Voiceover**:
> "क्रिटिकल मरीज़ों को 4 घंटे में एनडीआरएफ़ निकाल लिया। प्रिया के कैंप में कोई भी मौत नहीं हुई।"

*"Critical patients evacuated by NDRF within 4 hours. Zero preventable deaths at Priya's camp."*

**Screen recording**: QR code sync — shows triage data being transferred via QR mesh

> "QR कोड के ज़रिए एनडीआरएफ़ टीम को मरीज़ डेटा भेजा। बिना इंटरनेट। बिना बात किए।"

*"Patient data shared with NDRF via QR code. No internet. No words needed."*

**Visual**: Animated India map with glowing dots in disaster-prone regions
- Bihar, Uttarakhand, Odisha, Gujarat, Assam — dots light up

**Stat cards animate in**:
```

**Voiceover**:

*"Rakshak AI — India's disaster protection shield. When the network fails, AI saves."*

**Show**: The app works on a budget Samsung Galaxy M05 (₹8,999 phone)

> "₹9,000 के फ़ोन पर भी काम करता है। 4GB रैम। कोई GPU नहीं। कोई इंटरनेट नहीं।"

*"Works on a ₹9,000 phone. 4 GB RAM. No GPU. No internet."*

```
जब नेटवर्क नहीं बचाता, तो AI बचाता है

## Production Notes

| Element | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| **Screen recording** | Use real app on Galaxy M05 (or similar budget Samsung) |
| **Voice** | Hindi voiceover with clear, emotional delivery |
| **Subtitles** | English burned in + YouTube CC in Hindi |
| **Music** | Free track: "Rising" by Zakhar Valaha (epidemic sound) or similar |
| **Tools** | DaVinci Resolve (free) for editing, OBS for recording |
| **Thumbnail** | Priya holding phone with Rakshak AI open, flood background, tricolor overlay |
| **Duration** | 2:30 max (Samsung limit) |

### Equipment Needed
- Budget Samsung phone for screen recording
- Good mic (phone lapel mic is fine)
- DaVinci Resolve (free) or CapCut (free, mobile)

### File Deliverable
- MP4, 1920×1080, H.264, <50 MB
- Upload to Google Drive for submission link
