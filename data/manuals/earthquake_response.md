# Earthquake Response Protocol

## Immediate Actions (First 10 minutes)
1. Ensure personal safety — check for structural hazards
2. Conduct START triage on all casualties
3. Focus on RED (immediate) patients first
4. Establish casualty collection point in open area

## Medical Priorities
- Airway management for unconscious patients
- Control severe bleeding with tourniquets
- Immobilize suspected spinal injuries
- Treat crush injuries — watch for crush syndrome

## Communication
- Use mesh sync to share casualty data between teams
- Mark searched buildings with spray paint or chalk
- Report structural hazards immediately
