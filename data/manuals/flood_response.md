# Flood Response Protocol

## Immediate Actions
1. Move casualties to high ground immediately
2. Prioritize rescue of those in water or unstable structures
3. Treat hypothermia — remove wet clothing, warm gradually
4. Watch for drowning, head injuries, electrocution

## Medical Considerations
- High risk of waterborne disease (cholera, leptospirosis)
- Wound cleaning essential — contaminated water causes infection
- Respiratory distress from near-drowning requires monitoring
- Snake bites common in flood waters

## Evacuation
- Use boats, rafts, or elevated paths
- Mark safe routes with visible markers
- Establish shelter points on high ground with latrines
