# Heatwave Response Protocol

## Immediate Actions
1. Move casualties to cool/shaded area immediately
2. Remove excess clothing, apply cool water to skin
3. Fan aggressively to promote evaporative cooling
4. Provide oral rehydration if conscious

## Triage Considerations
- Heat stroke: RED (immediate) — hot dry skin, altered mental status
- Heat exhaustion: YELLOW (delayed) — heavy sweating, weakness, nausea
- Heat cramps: GREEN (minimal) — muscle pain, normal vitals

## Prevention
- Ensure continuous water supply at aid stations
- Schedule work/evacuation during cooler hours
- Monitor for signs of heat illness in all personnel
